Here’s the complete list of features for an enhanced inventory management system:

---

### **Basic Features**
- **Product Management**: Add, edit, and delete products.  
- **Stock Management**: Track stock levels, update stock quantities.  
- **Selling Option**: Sell products, manage sales transactions.  
- **Restock Option**: Manage restocking, track inventory replenishment.  
- **Sales Invoices**: Generate sales invoices, manage invoice history.  

---

### **Inventory Management**  
- **Stock Tracking**: Track stock levels, receive low-stock alerts.  
- **Inventory Reporting**: Generate inventory reports, track inventory movement.  
- **Barcode Scanning**: Scan barcodes to quickly add products to sales transactions.  
- **Serial Number Tracking**: Track serial numbers for products with unique identifiers.  
- **Batch Tracking**: Track batches of products, manage batch-specific data.  
- **Multi-Warehouse Support**: Manage inventory across multiple warehouses or locations.  
- **Inventory Categorization**: Organize inventory into categories, tags, or custom attributes.  
- **Reorder Point Alerts**: Automatically set and manage reorder points for products.  
- **Deadstock Management**: Identify and manage slow-moving or obsolete inventory.  
- **Expiration Date Tracking**: Track expiration dates for perishable or time-sensitive products.  

---

### **Customer Management**  
- **Customer Database**: Store customer information, track customer history.  
- **Customer Balance**: Track customer balances, manage customer accounts.  
- **Customer Remainder**: Send reminders to customers for overdue payments.  
- **Customer Loyalty Program**: Manage customer loyalty programs, track rewards.  
- **Customer Communication**: Send notifications, emails, and SMS to customers.  

---

### **Supplier Management**  
- **Supplier Database**: Store and manage supplier information.  
- **Purchase Orders**: Create and track purchase orders with suppliers.  
- **Supplier Performance Tracking**: Evaluate supplier performance based on delivery time, quality, and pricing.  

---

### **Sales and Reporting**  
- **Sales Reporting**: Generate sales reports, track sales performance.  
- **Product Reporting**: Generate product reports, track product performance.  
- **Customer Reporting**: Generate customer reports, track customer behavior.  
- **Inventory Valuation**: Calculate inventory valuation, track inventory costs.  
- **Tax Reporting**: Generate tax reports, manage tax compliance.  

---

### **Shipping and Logistics**  
- **Shipping Integration**: Integrate with shipping providers for real-time rate calculations and shipment tracking.  
- **Logistics Management**: Plan and optimize delivery routes for efficient shipping.  
- **Returns Management**: Manage product returns, process refunds, or restocking.  

---

### **Data Analytics**  
- **Sales Analytics**: Analyze sales data, track sales trends.  
- **Product Analytics**: Analyze product data, track product trends.  
- **Customer Analytics**: Analyze customer data, track customer behavior.  
- **Inventory Analytics**: Analyze inventory data, track inventory trends.  
- **Forecasting**: Use data analytics to forecast sales, inventory, and customer behavior.  
- **Customizable Dashboards**: Create custom dashboards to track KPIs and metrics.  
- **Heatmaps**: Visualize product demand by location or time period.  
- **Profit Margin Analysis**: Analyze profit margins for individual products or categories.  
- **Predictive Analytics**: Use machine learning to predict trends and optimize inventory decisions.  

---

### **Automation Features**  
- **Automated Restocking**: Automatically generate purchase orders for low-stock items.  
- **Workflow Automation**: Set up custom workflows for inventory and sales processes.  
- **AI-Powered Recommendations**: Use AI to recommend optimal stock levels or new products to stock.  

---

### **Mobile Accessibility**  
- **Mobile App**: Access inventory management features on mobile devices.  
- **Offline Mode**: Work offline and sync data once connected to the internet.  
- **Push Notifications**: Receive real-time alerts and updates on stock, sales, or supplier activities.  

---

### **Security and Access Control**  
- **User Management**: Manage user accounts, track user activity.  
- **Role-Based Access**: Control access to features and data based on user roles.  
- **Data Encryption**: Encrypt sensitive data, protect against unauthorized access.  
- **Audit Trail**: Track all system activity, maintain an audit trail.  
- **Backup and Recovery**: Automatically backup data, ensure quick recovery in case of data loss.  
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**: Add an extra layer of security for user logins.  
- **Geofencing**: Restrict system access to specific physical locations.  
- **Fraud Detection**: Use algorithms to detect unusual activity or discrepancies.  

---

### **Integration and API**  
- **API Integration**: Integrate with third-party APIs, enable seamless data exchange.  
- **E-commerce Integration**: Integrate with e-commerce platforms, manage online sales.  
- **Accounting Integration**: Integrate with accounting software, manage financial data.  
- **Payment Gateway Integration**: Integrate with payment gateways, manage online payments.  
- **Custom Integration**: Enable custom integrations, support unique business requirements.  

---

### **Employee and Vendor Collaboration**  
- **Task Management**: Assign tasks related to inventory management to team members.  
- **Vendor Portal**: Allow suppliers to update product availability and track order statuses.  

---

### **Sustainability Tracking**  
- **Waste Management**: Track and minimize inventory waste.  
- **Eco-Friendly Products**: Identify and track eco-friendly or sustainable inventory.  
- **Carbon Footprint Analysis**: Measure and report the environmental impact of inventory-related activities.  

---

### **Support and Assistance**  
- **Chatbot Assistance**: Provide AI-driven help within the system for common queries.  
- **User Training**: Offer tutorials or training modules for new users.  
- **Live Support**: Provide instant live support through chat or calls.
